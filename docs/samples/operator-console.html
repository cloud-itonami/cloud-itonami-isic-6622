Action gate (Insurance Intermediation Governor)

HARD holds cannot be overridden. Commission rates are independently checked against the jurisdiction's own recorded cap, never trusted from the proposal; placement binding requires at least two compared quotes.

:placement/intakeauto-commit when clean, no capital risk

:conflict/screenALWAYS human approval · HARD hold on any conflict-of-interest hit
:placement/bindALWAYS human approval · places real coverage (actuation/bind), ≥2 quotes required
:commission/bookALWAYS human approval · books a real commission (actuation/book-commission), rate independently checked against jurisdiction cap
