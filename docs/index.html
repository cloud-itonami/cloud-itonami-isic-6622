Activities of insurance agents and brokers

An open-source business blueprint for licensed operators

A forkable business and runtime for independent insurance intermediation. Deploy a governed Broker-LLM actor that drafts placement intake, validates compliance, screens for conflicts of interest, and proposes policy binding and commission booking — with an immutable audit ledger and no autonomous actuation on high-stakes decisions.

Built on the same StateGraph runtime and actor pattern as the life insurance, non-life insurance, and loss adjustment blueprints in this organization. The core contract: the Broker-LLM never binds a placement or books a commission that the Insurance Intermediation Governor would reject, and never does either without a human sign-off.

What's included

  • Insurance Intermediation Governor: 4 hard compliance checks (spec-basis, conflict-of-interest, insufficient-quotes, commission-rate-cap) + phase-gated workflow
  • Broker-LLM Advisor: drafts placement intake, assessment, conflict screening, binding and commission proposals
  • Append-only audit ledger for every intake, assessment, screening, binding and booking decision
  • Per-jurisdiction licensing and commission-cap requirement catalog with spec-based citations
  • Tested actor runtime via langgraph-clj (portable .cljc, ClojureScript + JVM)
